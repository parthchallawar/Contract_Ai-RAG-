Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of January 1, 2024, by and between NexusCorp Solutions (the "Provider") and Global Logistics Inc. (the "Client").

1. Scope of Work

Provider shall perform the services described in the various Statements of Work ("SOW") executed from time to time by both parties. Each SOW shall be governed by the terms of this Agreement.

2. Limitation of Liability warning

EXCEPT FOR INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS HEREUNDER, IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY INDIRECT, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES. THE PROVIDER'S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID BY CLIENT IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

3. Intellectual Property

All Deliverables created specifically for Client under an SOW shall be considered "work made for hire" and shall be the sole property of Client upon full payment of all fees due to Provider. Provider retains all rights in its pre-existing materials and methodologies.

4. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any legal action arising out of this Agreement shall be brought in the state or federal courts located in Wilmington, Delaware.

5. Indemnification

Provider agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Client from any and all third-party claims arising from Provider's breach of representations and warranties or gross negligence.
